### Contribution Rules

1. **Dates:**  
   Hacktoberfest runs from **October 1st UTC to October 31st UTC**.

2. **Contribution Complexity:**  
   Each contribution is rated by complexity and will earn you raffle entries as follows:

   - **Easy:** 1 entry
   - **Medium:** 2 entries
   - **Hard:** 3 entries
   - **Advanced:** 4 entries

3. **New Issue Submission:**  
   If you identify and submit a valid new issue (such as a bug report or a new component request, including your own design if you're a designer), you'll earn **1 additional entry**.

4. **Raffle System & Entry Limit:**
   Each contribution earns entries based on its difficulty, but for fairness, a maximum of 5 entries per user will be counted towards the raffle. You can contribute as much as you want, but only 5 of your entries will be added to the raffle system.

The winner will be selected randomly from all entries.

5. **Submission Deadline:**  
   All contributions must be submitted by **October 31, 11:59 PM UTC**.

6. **Payment Eligibility:**  
   To qualify for the **$100 reward**, you must be able to **receive international payments** via platforms like PayPal.

7. **Others:**
   - The reward will be paid within 30 days of the contest end date.
   - The reward is non-transferable and non-exchangeable.
   - Only contributions made during the Hacktoberfest event (October 1 to October 31) will count.
   - Contributions must follow the Hacktoberfest guidelines (e.g., no spammy PRs)
